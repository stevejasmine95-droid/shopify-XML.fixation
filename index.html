Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my Shopify store not showing up in search?
This often happens due to missing tags, incorrect URLs, or outdated media paths. It can also occur if your store's XML wasn't regenerated after theme customization or product uploads.
Can this really fix my indexing issues?
Yes — once your XML and sitemap are restructured properly, your store regains visibility and credibility with search engines. That directly improves discoverability and organic traffic.
